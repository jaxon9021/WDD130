RAF SIMONS

Raf Simons is a Belgian designer born on January 12 1968. Raf began in furniture design and moved to clothing with the launch of his label in 1995. Raf quickly grew as a designer in the fashion scene and was very repsected by many other designers and labels. While still designing for his own brand Raf designed for the likes of Jil Sander, Dior, Calvin Klein, and is now working with Prada all while continuing his brand. The label RAF SIMONS started and the shows focused on a European Punk aesthetic which was not really seen in the fashion industry at this time. Raf is one of the most known designers with pieces from his collections being scene everywhere from rappers such as kanye west and asap rocky to being heard about in music and songs.
